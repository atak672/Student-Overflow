Student Overflow was formed to help alleviate the overcrowding of Computer Science departments and courses through peer-led academic support at Duke University.

In courses with 400+ students, it can be challenging to find quality help due to limited office hours and a low staff-to-student ratio. We aim to expand the assistance that Teaching Assistants can provide by offering virtual office hours and one-on-one tutoring sessions, helping students get the guidance they need.

If you have any questions or are interested in applying to be a tutor, please visit the Contact Us page. In your message, make the subject "Application" and include basic information: name, year in school, major, and field of expertise. We look forward to starting the process with you!

Join us for office hours, Monday–Friday, 6 PM–9 PM. Please go to the "Office Hours" tab for access.
